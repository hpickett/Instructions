Lets get started.

Context

Please check and make sure your have the proper system requirements pior to starting downloading.

Requirements

  • Computers with proper system requirements

  • Internet access

Download Instructions

  1. Proceed to LibreOffice's website, http://www.libreoffice.org
  2. Please click on the "Download Now" option on the homepage.
  3. When prompted to the next page select "Download Version 4.3"
  4. When prompted select "save file"

    5. Once you select save file it will begin to download the installer in your internet downlaods.

  5. Once the download is complete click on the link, in downloads, for the installation wizard to open, and click "run".
  6. Follow the installation wizard by selecting next.

    7. Be sure to read each step carefully and for our case the typical download is reccomended.

  7. Follow the final steps and when promted press finish.
